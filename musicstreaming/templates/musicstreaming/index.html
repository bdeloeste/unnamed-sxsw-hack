Why TuneFork?
Plug In Inspiration

TuneFork encourages you to collabotate with artists from all over the country and to stimulate your musical perception. Our community is comprised of musicians that are eager to inspire and to be inspired. TurnFork members have instant access to artists tracks and have the ability to extract sub-comonents of each track. Whether it be vocals, guitar, or piano recordings, TuneFork allows easy extraction of track segments.

Collaboration Made Easy

Have you ever wanted to pull the instramentals out of your favorite song and mix them into something new? TurnFork allows you to pull any component of a subscribed artist's song and utilize them in your own personal digital audio workstation. After your contribution, TuneFork allows you to share your remixed track with all TuneFork artists.



Grow As A Musician

TuneFork is targeted specifically to a network indie artists who are looking to collaborate and help each other branch out into different genres. Music is all about exploration and finding your true passion as an artist, which makes the TuneFork community the perfect setting for beginning artists to put themselves out there are work with other aspiring musicians.


Spread The Love of Music

If TuneFork is for you, it's because you are passionate about any and all melodies and the way the vibration of music makes you feel. No matter where you are in your music career, there's always time to tune into an unfamiliar artist. Whether you are a novice or a legend, TuneFork has a special spot for you to spread your musical vision and insight.
